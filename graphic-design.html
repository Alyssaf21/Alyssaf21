About

Hi! I'm Alyssa, an aspiring user interface and experience designer.

I like to think of the creative challenges presented by my work as a combination of two of my favorite things: puzzles and art. Whether it's by overhauling a site, designing a framework for organizing office supplies or rearranging the furniture in my house, I'm always looking for ways to improve the systems I come into contact with.

Work aside, I also have a great love for psychology, philosophy, hand-drawn animation, and wacky puzzle games.

Drop me a line or request a resume at a.c.fusato@gmail.com!
